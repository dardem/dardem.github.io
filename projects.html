TextDetox — Multilingual Text Detoxification

A shared task and dataset for parallel text detoxification spanning 9 languages including German, Chinese, Arabic, Hindi, and Amharic. Includes the PAN 2024 multilingual detoxification challenge and Chain-of-Thought–inspired prompting methods.

GitHub
📰

Cross-lingual Fake News Detection

A system leveraging multilingual evidence to improve fake news detection. The key insight: true news is repeated with consistent facts across languages, while misinformation diverges. Developed with a focus on the Russian invasion of Ukraine.

Paper
😤

HatePRISM — Hate Speech Proactive Mitigation

A comprehensive framework examining hate speech regulations across countries, social platform policies, and NLP research datasets. Reveals inconsistencies in hate speech definitions and proposes unified research directions.

Paper
🌍

Low-Resource NLP Tutorial

A hands-on tutorial designed for NLP practitioners working with multilingual and low-resource languages. Covers end-to-end pipelines for building equitable and socially impactful language technologies.
