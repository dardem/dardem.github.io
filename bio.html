About me and my research journey

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Social Computing Research Group at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), Germany, where I work under the supervision of Prof. Georg Groh. I received my PhD from the Skolkovo Institute of Science and Technology (Skoltech), Russia, where I was advised by Prof. Alexander Panchenko. My doctoral dissertation focused on methods for detecting and combating harmful multilingual textual content, with a particular emphasis on text detoxification and fake news detection.
