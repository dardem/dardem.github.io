Bio

About me and my professional journey

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Data Science and Analytics Group at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), Germany, where I work under the supervision of Prof. Alex Fraser. I received my PhD from the Skolkovo Institute of Science and Technology (Skoltech), where I was advised by Prof. Alexander Panchenko. My doctoral dissertation focused on Methods for Fighting Harmful Multilingual Textual Content, with a particular emphasis on combating toxic speech and fake news detection. Currently, I advocate for developing AI and Language Technologies for Social Good.

Bio for Talks: Dr. Daryna Dementieva is a Responsible AI and Language Technologies researcher at Technical University of Munich. She received her PhD in 2022 with the topic Methods for Fighting Harmful Multilingual Textual Content developing multilingual NLP methods to combat toxic speech and fake news. Now, she continues her research with overall direction of NLP for Social Good. Dr. Dementieva is the author of more than 50 papers with several awards, one of them - ACL2025 best resource paper award - as well as the organiser of NLP for Positive Impact Workshop and LLMs with Limited Resources shared tasks. She tirelessly advocates for the development of responsible language technologies that will truly serve many communities for good.
